Privacy Policy – HalaGo

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to HalaGo. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using HalaGo, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, process payments, and ensure a fair environment free of fraud, we collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Information (Provided by You):

  • Account Data: Name and Email address (collected via registration or Google Sign-In).
  • Payment Data: Wallet addresses (e.g., Binance Pay ID, MTN and Syriatel number, Sham Cash wallet address) provided by you strictly for the purpose of processing withdrawals.

B. Device and Technical Information (Automatically Collected):

  • Device Identifiers: We collect your Device ID and Google Advertising ID (GAID) to track offer completion accurately and prevent multiple account creation on the same device.
  • Network Data: We collect your IP Address to verify your country and detect the use of VPNs or Proxies (Anti-Fraud Security).
  • Device Details: Model, Manufacturer, and Operating System version to ensure app compatibility.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To create your account and manage your points balance.
  • Payment Processing: To accurately send your earnings to the wallet details you provided.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and block suspicious activities, such as using Emulators, VPNs, or bots.
  • Third-Party Verification: To communicate with our advertising partners to confirm that you have successfully completed a task.

4. Third-Party Services (Offerwalls)

HalaGo integrates with third-party providers (Offerwalls) such as Ovnix, Flexwall, Playtime, and others to provide earning opportunities. These third parties may collect information directly from you to validate your tasks.

Disclaimer: We do not control the privacy practices of these third parties. We strongly encourage you to review their privacy policies before engaging with their offers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary. In compliance with Google Play policies, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and data at any time. You can request this by contacting us at our support email. We will process your deletion request within 30 days.

6. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

HalaGo is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
